AppStore

These apps are mostly the result of my studying JavaScript. They usually started life as useful exercises to show how the code might be used in real life situations but, although learning the the code was the most important element, the design of the apps was, in most cases, fairly rudimentary. So I have redesigned many of them to make the look and feel more user-friendly. I have also developed my own apps such as the Print Generator, the Code Overview, the Latest News app and the Calculator of Things.

Try them out and if you find some of them useful, make this page a favourite and they will be easily accessible.
